Sponsorship Opportunities

Girl Develop IT Pittsburgh needs your help! If you are interested in becoming a sponsor, please contact us at sponsorship@girldevelopitpgh.com.

Benefits

Here are some benefits of becoming a sponsor:

  • Helping people learn to program and become more involved in the Pittsburgh technology community
  • Logo and link on our website
  • Recognition by our organization (tweets, email, word-of-mouth, etc. - as deemed appropriate by organizers)

Suggestions

Below are some ways you can help Girl Develop IT Pittsburgh. We are open to suggestions! If you have other ideas for how you can support Girl Develop IT Pittsburgh, please let us know by emailing sponsorship@girldevelopitpgh.com.

  • Space for classes - this is our primary need right now
  • Donating resources for classes
  • Laptops for students without them
  • Electronics (e.g. microcontrollers) for hardware-related classes Printed resources for classes (binders, printed materials, etc.)
  • Space for meetup nights (code & coffee, hack nights, etc.)
  • Food and/or drinks for meetup nights (code & coffee, hack nights, etc.)
  • Full or partial scholarships for students who cannot afford classes (classes are $80 per student)
